OPCI story

In 2010 we decided to construct our Egyptian National pharmaceutical company with international standards in the new orientation. Not to be me-too products but to add value to the pharmaceutical market facing the challenges and satisfying the market with highly needed pharmaceutical products.

We started with our first challenge to introduce desmopressin acetate the (ADH hormone analog) with know-how in its registration and we succeeded to overcome this challenge and introduced Omegapress in 2019 which fills the gap in the treatment of central diabetes insipidus, nocturnal enuresis, and Nocturia.

Omegacough (levodropropizine) the first peripheral antitussive that does not affect the cough center is a safe product for children.

Dostilact (Cabergoline) reduces the level of prolactin hormone that may cause infertility. and we still have more than 35 products in our pipeline to be introduced one by one.

OMEGAPRESS (desmopressin acetate)

  • is an antidiuretic hormone analog it is like a natural hormone but with no pressor effect and a longer duration of action.
  • It is bioequivalent to the original brand and it is the only competitor available in the Egyptian and almost middle east markets.

OMEGAPRESS prescribed in cases of

  • Nocturnal enuresis.
  • Nocturnal polyuria.
  • Central diabetes insipidus.

OMEGAPRESS is available

  • In two concentrations 0.1 mg and 0.2 mg.
  • In cartoon package (30 Tablets) contains three ALU/ALU strips each containing 10 tablets.
  • At a cost-effective price.

DOSTILACT (Cabergoline 0.5 mg)

DOSTILACT (Cabergoline 0.5 mg) is a dopamine agonist It is bioequivalent to the original brand.
It is introduced to the market in a proper amber glass jar container like the original brand the most convenient and suitable container keeping the stability of Cabergoline.

DOSTILACT prescribed in cases of

  • Anovulation and infertility due to hyperprolactinemia.
  • Amenorrhea and Dysmenorrhea.
  • Prophylaxis against Ovarian Hyper-Stimulation Syndrome (OHSS) in IVF.
  • Ablactation even primary or secondary.
  • Male infertility or loss of libido due to hyperprolactinemia.

DOSTILACT is available

  • In 0.5 mg tablet in glass jar contains 2 tablets.
  • At a cost-effective price.

OMEGACOUGH (Levodropropizine 30mg/5ml)

OMEGACOUGH (Levodropropizine 30mg/5ml) is a peripheral antitussive that effective and safe for the treatment of dry cough in all ages from children above 2 years to the elderly. It introduces high efficacy equivalent and almost more than centrally acting antitussive with high safety without side effects common for centrally acting antitussive as Drowsiness, Respiratory depression, Constipation, Physical dependence.

OMEGACOUGH prescribed in cases of suffering Dry cough like

  • Cough variant asthma.
  • Upper respiratory tract infections.
  • Smokers.
  • Chest tumors.
  • GERD (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease).
  • Plural diseases.

OMEGACOUGH is available

  • In a palatable flavored Syrup (bottle 120ml)

Omegapredine (Dalfampridine 10mg)

Omegapredine (Dalfampridine 10mg) Treatment to improve walking in patients with multiple sclerosis.

Rofloair (Roflumilast 0.5 mg)

Rofloair (Roflumilast 0.5 mg) Orally administration drug for the treatment of inflammatory conditions Of the lungs such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).
