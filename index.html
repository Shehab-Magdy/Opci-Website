OPCI story

Since 2010 we decided to construct our Egyptian National pharmaceutical company with international standards in new orientation. Not to be me too products but to add value to the pharmaceutical market facing the challenges and satisfying the market with highly needed pharmaceutical products . Then we started with our first challenge to introduce desmopressin acetate the (ADH hormone analogue) with knowhow in its registration and we succeeded to overcome this challenge and introduced Omegapress in 2019 which fill the gap in treatment of central diabetes insipidus, Nocturnal enuresis and Nocturia.

Then Omegacough (levodropropizine) the first peripheral antitussive that not affect cough center to be safe product for children. Dostilact (Cabergoline) that reduces the level of prolactin hormone that may cause infertility. and we still have more than 35 products in our pipeline to be introduced one by one.

OMEGAPRESS (desmopressin acetate)

  • is an antidiuretic hormone analog it is like natural hormone but with no pressor effect and longer duration of action.
  • It is bioequivalent to the original brand Minirin the product of FERRING company and it is the only competitor available in Egyptian and almost middle east market due to its difficulty and special requirements in manufacturing.
  • Keep company morals and ethics.
  • Prepare good calibers.

OMEGAPRESS prescribed in cases of

  • Nocturnal enuresis.
  • Nocturnal polyuria.
  • Central diabetes insipidus.

OMEGAPRESS Available in two concentrations 0.1 mg and 0.2 mg

OMEGAPRESS introduced in cartoon package (30 Tablets) contains three aluminum strips each contains 10 tablets.

OMEGAPRESS available in a cost-effective price almost 50% less than MINIRIN.

DOSTILACT (Cabergoline 0.5 mg)

DOSTILACT (Cabergoline 0.5 mg) is a dopamine agonist It is bioequivalent to the original brand Dostinex the product of Pfizer company. It is introduced to the market in a proper amber glass jar container like the original brand the most convenient and suitable container keeping the stability of Cabergoline.

DOSTILACT prescribed in cases of

  • Anovulation and infertility due to hyperprolactinemia.
  • Amenorrhea and Dysmenorrhea.
  • Prophylaxis against Ovarian Hyper-Stimulation Syndrome (OHSS) in IVF.
  • Ablactation even primary or secondary.
  • Male infertility or loss of libido due to hyperprolactinemia.

DOSTILACT Available in 0.5 mg tablet in glass jar contains 2 tablets.

DOSTILACT Available in a cost-effective price almost 60% less than Dostinex.

OMEGACOUGH (Levodropropizine 30mg/5ml)

OMEGACOUGH (Levodropropizine 30mg/5ml) is a peripheral antitussive that effective and safe for the treatment of dry cough in all ages from 2 to elderly. It introduces high efficacy equivalent and almost more than centrally acting antitussive with high safety without side effects common for centrally acting antitussive as Drowsiness, Respiratory depression, Constipation, Physical dependence.

OMEGACOUGH prescribed in cases of suffering Dry cough like

  • Cough variant asthma.
  • Upper respiratory tract infections.
  • Smokers.
  • Chest tumors.
  • GERD.
  • Plural diseases.

OMEGACOUGH Available in Strawberry flavored Syrup (bottle 120ml)
